Why Coding?

During my time at The University of Texas at Austin, I thought that science was my "calling", but throughout my time there, I always felt I hadn't found the right career path. I took the science route because that's what everyone in my family wanted me to do. I've always loved technology but stuck with the science route because I didn't want to disappoint my parents. A couple months ago, I came across a YouTube video about coding and that was the start of my coding journey. It was amazing and I instantly fell in love with everything about coding. Every day I strive to improve my existing knowledge and learn new technologies and languages. I just really want to continue to gain and consume as much knowledge as possible every single day.
